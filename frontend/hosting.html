Hosting Guidelines

A few ground rules to keep AtOurs safe, trustworthy, and fun for every guest. Please review these before creating or publishing an event.

Accurate Listings
  • Use a real photo of the venue or a relevant poster — no stock images that misrepresent the event.
  • Describe the event honestly: vibe, activities, and what's included in the ticket price.
  • Keep the date, time, and location up to date. Notify guests immediately if anything changes.
  • Set an accurate max guest count and age limit so bookings match what your venue can actually handle.
Safety & Conduct
  • Provide a safe, accessible venue and be reachable to guests on the day of the event.
  • Clearly list entry rules (ID checks, dress code, house rules) in the Rules field.
  • No hosting of illegal activity, hate speech, harassment, or discriminatory entry conditions.
  • Alcohol or age-restricted content must be flagged with an appropriate age limit.
Pricing & Cancellations
  • Set ticket prices fairly and disclose any extra costs guests should expect at the door.
  • If you cancel or reschedule, update the event and inform booked guests as early as possible.
  • Refund guests promptly for events you cancel, per your stated cancellation policy.
Not Allowed
  • Fake or duplicate listings, bait-and-switch pricing, or events that never take place.
  • Any content that is sexually explicit, violent, or promotes harm to guests.
  • Collecting guest payments or personal data outside the platform.

Listings that break these guidelines may be removed, and repeat violations can lead to your hosting privileges being suspended. Questions? Reach out via the Support page.
